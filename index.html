Learn more about FriendLink Social

FriendLink Social is a new social media app that lets you share wide snaps and stickaboos with your friends and family. It is a fast, fun, and safe way to connect with the people you care about.

Features:

  • Wide Snap Camera: Capture wide snaps in portrait mode and share them on FriendLink Social.
  • Stickaboos: Express yourself with Stickaboos, create and share stickers, photos, screenshots, and much more.
  • Call: Stay in touch with friends by directly calling them from your contact list instead of using a third-party provider.
  • Reactmoji: See your friend's reaction to your wide snaps and stickaboos. React to your friend's wide snaps and stickaboos with reactmoji.
  • Customize: You can customize the app theme according to your choice! Once you choose app theme, app borders and layouts will be changed to the color of your choice. FriendLink social allows you to change the capture button (animated capture buttons).
  • Filter Less / Filter Free / Unfiltered: FriendLink Social promotes unfiltered content and doesn't have any filters in it to make your snaps picture perfect!
  • Safe to Use: FriendLink Social comes with an inbuilt NSFW content filter that works both offline and online with an accuracy of 99.99%, making it one of the safest social media platforms to work with.
