About the Creator

Mr. Siddhartha N. Manmothe

THE CREATOR AND GRIEVANCE OFFICER

Meet Mr.Siddhartha N. Manmothe, an ambitious individual currently pursuing a B.S degree, and the visionary behind FriendLink Social - a cutting-edge social media platform designed to provide a space for individuals to share unfiltered images and thoughts with their friends. Unlike other social media platforms, FriendLink Social was, is and will be algorithm-free, which means that users can see all the content posted by their friends without any interference from a sorting algorithm. Moreover, the platform offers a completely private social experience, ensuring that only invited friends can view the content posted, and a No Short Format Video experience as well. As the grievance officer of FriendLink Social, Mr. Manmothe ensures that the platform is a safe and inclusive space for all users. Designed to minimize time spent on the app, FriendLink Social is user-friendly and only takes five minutes to check what your friends have shared, instead of spending an exhausting 11 hours scrolling through your feed. With a personalized app theme, a focus on authenticity and transparency, and an unwavering commitment to user privacy, FriendLink Social is revolutionizing the social media landscape, one connection at a time.
